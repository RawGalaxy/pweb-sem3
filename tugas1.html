Quick Introduction

Horimiya, or Hori-san to Miyamura-kun, is a japanese adaptation of the webcomic series of the
same name created by the same author, HERO. The anime began in early January 2021 with
Cloverworks as it's studio. This series revolves around the two main characters, Kyouko Hori and
Izumi Miyamura, where the two of them fall in love with them sharing their secrets at first.
The series also provides other character's love like Yuki Yoshikawa and Tooru Ishikawa, Kakeru Sengoku and Remi Ayasaki, and others.
